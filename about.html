About Me / Über Mich / Om Mig

My name is Tommy and I am a 36-year-old man from Gothenburg in Sweden. I recently moved to Hamburg in Germany to live with my girlfriend, our two cats and to fulfil my dream towards a career within the IT-sector. From my early 20s to a few years ago I ran a family business together with my father. We decided to close the company a couple a years ago, which opened the possibility for me to pursue the dream I have had since I was young - to work in the IT- sector. To achieve this, I began an education at the code institute. My goal is to become a full stack web developer. In my spare time I enjoy cooking, traveling, gaming, and of course spending time with my girlfriend and our friends.
